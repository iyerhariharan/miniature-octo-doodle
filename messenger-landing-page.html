Frequently Asked Questions

How does verification work?
Every company goes through KYB (Know Your Business) verification. We verify company registration, domain ownership, and authorized representatives before granting access.

Messenger is built for professional B2B communication between institutions. Your existing Telegram chats remain separate—use Messenger for verified business communication.
